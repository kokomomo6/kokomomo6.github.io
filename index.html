A delightful exchange programme

Last week, there were some overseas students who went on a trip to visit our school. We had a great time! Let me tell you more about our trip. We had to pick them up at the airport. I giggled and even hummed a tune while waiting for them. Suddenly, right out of the corner of my eye, I saw four foreigners walking in our direction. They must be the overseas students! They seemed quite friendly. We greeted them and they greeted us back. Soon, we got to know each other. My partner was a British girl with long blond hair and bright blue eyes. Her name is Mabel. We got along quite well. Soon, it was lunchtime, so we decided to eat at a Chinese restaurant. We had lots of dim sum and chatted happily together. The students seemed to enjoy the food, too! Then, we went to the beach. We built sandcastles and swam in the sea. Everything was going smoothly until I realized that one of the students, Chris, was gone. We looked for him everywhere, but we couldn't find him. We were very worried. Suddenly, Harry saw a shark approaching! We screamed and yelled, but when it got closer, we realized that it was just an inflated shark balloon. Chris had pulled a prank on us! How naughty! On day two, they had some classes with us. They got along with the other students well. However, they had to leave for the plane at 6 pm sharp. We accompanied them to the airport and bid them farewell. It was a nice experience. I enjoyed it very much.
